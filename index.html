Cyberpunk RED GIG PLANNER

A modular Cyberpunk RED gig planner with expandable sections, structured NPC and encounter tools, optional printable stat blocks, save/load support, and clean export formatting, built to turn rough ideas into table-ready jobs fast.
Inspired by the teachings of Deficient Master, as well as Marcus Pascall's QuarterShots, and Rob Mulligan's Quick Campaign Builder.
Created by bigulabot. If you'd like to support the project, buy me a coffee ☕!
